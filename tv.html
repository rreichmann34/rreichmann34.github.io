TV

I don't watch too much TV, but there are a few shows that have stood out over my life.

Avatar: The Last Airbender
An animated fantasy series following Aang, a young Airbender destined to master all four elements and bring peace to a world torn by war. Known for its deep themes, character development, and Eastern-inspired mythology.
Breaking Bad
A gripping drama centered around Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher who turns to manufacturing methamphetamine after a terminal cancer diagnosis. The show explores morality, identity, and consequence in stark detail.
Attack on Titan
A dark anime saga in which humanity fights for survival against giant humanoid creatures called Titans. As secrets unravel, the story dives into geopolitics, free will, and the nature of truth.
